ABOUT US

The Forage Finder is a resource for low-income neighborhood residents who struggle to afford basic groceries. Foraging was widely useful in the days of huntering and gathering, and still is useful in an enviornment as urban as a city.

With the development of the Forage Finder, users are able to document edible plant species and pinpoint their exact locations onto a geographical map. Image processing will be used to approve of the photo submission as well as forum discussions in order to confirm the species of the plant.

"I believe foraging and botany should be introduced to us from an early age."

National Geographic Journalist and Professional Forager: Wross Lawrence
